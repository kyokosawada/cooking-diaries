## Design Decisions
- All recipes are Easy difficulty, 10-30 min cook time
- Filipino recipes sourced from Panlasang Pinoy and Kawaling Pinoy
- Ingredient categories follow existing convention: Protein, Produce, Pantry, Sauces, Spices, Dairy, Grains, Other
- Each recipe includes YouTube video link and beginner tips
