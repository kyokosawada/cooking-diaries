## ASIAN RECIPES

### 1. Egg Fried Rice
**Cuisine:** Chinese | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Joshua Weissman
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2pfPNpFIKA4

**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups cooked, chilled white rice (day-old)
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tbsp unsalted butter
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp white vinegar
- 3 garlic cloves, grated
- 1-inch knob fresh ginger, grated
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 carrot, diced
- 1 bunch green onions, sliced
- 1 cup frozen peas
- Toasted sesame oil (optional)
- Kosher salt

**Steps:**
1. Cook rice ahead of time. Spread on a tray and refrigerate overnight (day-old rice fries better).
2. Whisk eggs with salt. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Scramble eggs, chop into pieces, set aside.
3. Mix sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and ginger in a small bowl.
4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet/wok over medium-high. Sauté carrots 2 min, add white parts of green onions 1 min, add frozen peas 1-2 min.
5. Add chilled rice, break up clumps. Cook 5 min, stirring regularly.
6. Pour sauce over rice, mix thoroughly.
7. Fold in scrambled eggs and green onion tops. Drizzle with sesame oil. Serve.

### 2. Lo Mein Noodles
**Cuisine:** Chinese | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** RecipeTin Eats
**YouTube:** https://www.recipetineats.com/lo-mein-noodles/ (video on page)

**Ingredients:**
- 500g fresh egg noodles
- 300g chicken breast or thigh, sliced thin
- 1½ tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ onion, sliced
- 2 carrots, cut into thin batons
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 6 green onions, cut into 2-inch lengths

**Sauce:**
- 4 tsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Chinese cooking wine (or mirin)
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ tsp sesame oil
- ¼ tsp white pepper
- ¼ cup water

**Steps:**
1. Mix all sauce ingredients until smooth.
2. Coat chicken with 2 tsp of the sauce. Set aside.
3. Cook noodles per packet. Drain.
4. Heat oil in wok over high heat. Add onion and garlic, stir 30 sec.
5. Add chicken, stir 1 min. Add carrots and bell pepper, cook 2 min.
6. Add noodles and remaining sauce. Toss 30 sec.
7. Add green onions, toss 1 min until noodles are glossy. Serve.

### 3. Hoisin Chicken Stir-Fry
**Cuisine:** Chinese | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Marion's Kitchen
**YouTube:** https://www.marionskitchen.com/hoisin-chicken-stir-fry/ (video on page)

**Ingredients:**
- 400g boneless chicken breast, sliced thin
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 onion, sliced into wedges
- 2 bell peppers, bite-sized pieces
- 3 spring onions, cut into batons

**Marinade:**
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- ½ tsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry)
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 3 tsp cornstarch

**Hoisin Sauce:**
- 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
- 3 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- ½ tsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tsp white vinegar
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1½ tsp black pepper

**Steps:**
1. Slice chicken thin against the grain. Mix with marinade, set aside 10 min.
2. Mix hoisin sauce ingredients in a bowl.
3. Heat oil in wok over high heat. Add onion, stir-fry 30 sec.
4. Add chicken, leave 3-4 min to form golden crust, then toss with onions.
5. Add garlic and bell peppers, stir-fry 30 sec.
6. Pour sauce around edges, stir-fry 30 sec.
7. Toss in spring onions. Serve over rice.

### 4. Gyudon (Japanese Beef Rice Bowl)
**Cuisine:** Japanese | **Time:** 10 min | **Source:** Adam Liaw
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mNzXoRiXxpM

**Ingredients:**
- 500g beef (scotch fillet or rump), very thinly sliced
- ¾ cup chicken stock or dashi
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sake
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 brown onion, thickly sliced
- 4 cups cooked short-grain rice
- 2 spring onions, sliced
- Pickled ginger (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Combine stock, soy sauce, sake, mirin, and sugar in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.
2. Add onion, simmer 2-3 min until softened.
3. Simmer rapidly ~10 min until liquid reduces by two-thirds.
4. Add beef, stir gently 1-2 min until just cooked.
5. Serve over rice bowls with spring onions and pickled ginger.

**Tip:** Ask your butcher to slice the beef thin, or buy pre-sliced shabu-shabu beef from an Asian grocery.

### 5. Pad See Ew (Thai Stir-Fried Noodles)
**Cuisine:** Thai | **Time:** 18 min | **Source:** RecipeTin Eats / Hot Thai Kitchen
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ohy1DELF4is

**Ingredients:**
- 200g dried wide rice noodles
- 150g boneless chicken thighs, sliced
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil (divided)
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 1 large egg
- 4 stems Chinese broccoli (or regular broccoli)

**Sauce:**
- 2 tsp dark soy sauce
- 1½ tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp light soy sauce
- 2 tsp white vinegar
- 2 tsp sugar

**Steps:**
1. Cook rice noodles per packet. Drain.
2. Mix sauce ingredients.
3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok over high heat. Add garlic 15 sec, then chicken. Cook until mostly done. Add broccoli stems, then leaves.
4. Push to side, crack egg in and scramble.
5. Remove everything to a plate.
6. Heat 2 tbsp oil in wok until smoking. Add noodles and sauce. Let sit and char 1-1.5 min (don't over-stir).
7. Return chicken mixture, toss together. Serve.

### 6. Korean Ground Beef Bulgogi Bowl
**Cuisine:** Korean | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Aaron and Claire
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s2RDzkr1zgE

**Ingredients:**
- 600g ground beef
- 2 shiitake mushrooms, finely diced
- ½ yellow onion, finely diced
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- ½ tbsp toasted sesame oil
- 5 cups cooked rice
- Sesame seeds

**Sauce:**
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp mirin
- 2½ tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
- Black pepper

**Steps:**
1. Mix sauce ingredients. Add ground beef and mushrooms, mix thoroughly.
2. Heat oil in wok over medium-high. Sauté onion whites and diced onion 3 min.
3. Add beef mixture. Cook 7-8 min, breaking up beef, until browned and liquid evaporates.
4. Off heat, add green onion tops and sesame oil.
5. Serve over rice with sesame seeds.

### 7. Fried Rice (Basics)
**Cuisine:** Chinese-American | **Time:** 25 min | **Source:** Basics with Babish
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dk0axPbD2pc

**Ingredients:**
- 4½ cups cooked, chilled rice (day-old)
- 2 eggs, beaten
- Vegetable oil
- ½ cup mushrooms, chopped
- ½ cup frozen peas
- 1 cup chopped ham (or shrimp/chicken)
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 3 tbsp oyster sauce
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- Scallions, sliced
- 1 tbsp butter
- Sesame oil
- Sesame seeds

**Steps:**
1. Prepare rice ahead — refrigerate overnight.
2. Scramble eggs in oiled pan. Set aside.
3. Cook protein until done. Set aside.
4. Sauté mushrooms 2 min, add peas.
5. Add garlic 30 sec.
6. Add rice. Pour mixed oyster sauce + soy sauce over rice.
7. Fry on high 3-5 min until hot and slightly crispy.
8. Return eggs and protein. Add sesame oil, scallions, sesame seeds, butter. Toss and serve.

### 8. Pasta Aglio e Olio
**Cuisine:** Italian | **Time:** 20 min | **Source:** Babish
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bJUiWdM__Qw

**Ingredients:**
- ½ lb linguine
- ½ head garlic, thinly sliced
- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- ½ cup flat-leaf parsley, chopped
- ½ lemon
- Kosher salt, black pepper

**Steps:**
1. Boil heavily salted water. Cook linguine 1 min less than package says. Reserve ¼ cup pasta water.
2. Heat olive oil in pan over medium. Add garlic, stir until golden (don't burn).
3. Add red pepper flakes, reduce heat.
4. Add drained pasta + reserved pasta water. Squeeze lemon, add parsley, toss.
5. Season and serve.

### 9. Grilled Cheese Sandwich
**Cuisine:** American | **Time:** 10 min | **Source:** Joshua Weissman
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WcGYBX6Ucvg

**Ingredients:**
- 2 slices white bread
- 2 tbsp unsalted butter, room temp
- 2 slices American cheese

**Steps:**
1. Heat nonstick pan over medium. Melt 1 tbsp butter.
2. Place bread in butter, top with both cheese slices, then second bread slice.
3. Reduce to medium-low. Toast until golden, ~2-3 min.
4. Add remaining butter, flip. Toast 2-3 more min.
5. Slice diagonally. Serve.

### 10. Fried Chicken Sandwich
**Cuisine:** American | **Time:** 20 min | **Source:** Ethan Chlebowski
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K4UW4kovH-c

**Ingredients:**
- 2 boneless chicken thighs
- 100g milk
- 1 tsp white vinegar
- 50g flour + 25g cornstarch
- ½ tsp each: salt, black pepper, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, oregano
- Neutral oil for frying (2 inches in pot)
- 2 tbsp mayo + 2 tbsp Dijon mustard + 1 tbsp hot sauce (sauce)
- 2 brioche buns, lettuce, pickled onions

**Steps:**
1. Soak chicken in milk + salt + vinegar.
2. Heat oil to 375°F/190°C in a deep pot.
3. Mix flour, cornstarch, and spices. Drizzle in some milk to create shaggy clumps.
4. Press chicken into dredge for thick coating.
5. Fry 8 min, flipping halfway. Internal temp 165°F/73°C.
6. Rest on paper towels, sprinkle salt.
7. Mix sauce. Assemble on buns with lettuce and pickled onions.

### 11. Tomato Soup + Grilled Cheese
**Cuisine:** American | **Time:** 30 min | **Source:** Adam Ragusea
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=r4bGuZSur0E

**Soup Ingredients:**
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 4 oz butter (1 stick)
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ¼ cup flour
- 2 cans (28 oz each) whole/crushed tomatoes
- Salt, sugar (if needed), water (to thin)

**Steps:**
1. Melt butter, cook onion 5 min. Add pepper.
2. Stir in flour, cook 1-2 min.
3. Add tomatoes, simmer 30 min.
4. Blend until smooth. Season with salt.
5. Make grilled cheese while soup simmers (see recipe #9 method).

### 12. 15-Min Alfredo with Peas
**Cuisine:** Italian-American | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Ethan Chlebowski
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Pz3zl2qE_vE

**Ingredients:**
- 227g penne pasta
- 56g butter (4 tbsp)
- 70g Parmigiano Reggiano, finely grated
- 1 garlic clove, grated
- 50g peas (frozen is fine)
- Fresh basil, sliced
- Salt

**Steps:**
1. Put penne in skillet, cover with hot water, pinch of salt.
2. High heat, stir first 60 sec. Set timer 8 min, stir occasionally.
3. Drain excess water, leaving a few tbsp in pan.
4. Add butter, stir until melted and emulsified.
5. Add Parmigiano, stir vigorously until creamy.
6. Mix in garlic, peas, basil. Serve.

### 13. Fettuccine al Burro (3-Ingredient Butter Pasta)
**Cuisine:** Italian | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Joshua Weissman
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WcGYBX6Ucvg

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb fettuccine
- ½ cup salted butter, cubed
- 1 cup Parmigiano Reggiano, finely grated

**Steps:**
1. Boil salted water. Cook fettuccine al dente (~7 min). Reserve ½ cup pasta water.
2. Drain pasta, return to pot (off heat).
3. Add butter, toss vigorously until melted and emulsified.
4. Splash pasta water as needed for silky consistency.
5. Stir in Parmigiano. Season with salt. Serve.

### 14. Chicken with Lemon Pan Sauce
**Cuisine:** French/American | **Time:** 25 min | **Source:** Babish
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hR6agVkRRUo

**Ingredients:**
- 2 chicken breasts (bone-in or boneless)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Kosher salt, black pepper
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- ¼ cup white wine (or sherry)
- ½ cup chicken stock
- 1 tsp fresh thyme, chopped
- 1 tsp fresh sage, chopped
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

**Steps:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F/200°C. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in oven-safe skillet over medium-high.
3. Sear chicken 3-4 min per side until golden.
4. Transfer skillet to oven 10-15 min (internal temp 160°F).
5. Remove chicken, rest on cutting board.
6. Return skillet to stovetop (careful, handle is hot). Sauté shallot 1 min.
7. Add wine, scrape up browned bits. Add stock, herbs. Reduce 3-4 min.
8. Off heat, stir in butter and lemon juice.
9. Slice chicken, spoon sauce over top. Serve.

### 15. Brazilian Coconut Chickpea Curry
**Cuisine:** Brazilian/Indian | **Time:** 25 min | **Source:** RecipeTin Eats
**YouTube:** https://youtu.be/jpQoJTMMJoc

**Ingredients:**
- 1½ tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
- 2 cans chickpeas, drained
- 400ml coconut milk
- 400ml can crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken/veg stock
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp cumin
- ½-1 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1½ tsp sugar
- ½ tsp salt
- 50g baby spinach
- 3 tbsp cilantro, chopped
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- Rice for serving

**Steps:**
1. Heat oil over high. Cook garlic and onion 1 min.
2. Add bell pepper, cook 2 min.
3. Add chickpeas, coconut milk, tomatoes, stock, spices, sugar, salt. Stir.
4. Simmer on medium-low 12-15 min until sauce turns orangey-red.
5. Stir in spinach until wilted. Add cilantro and lime juice.
6. Serve over rice.

### 17. Red Lentil Curry (Dal)
**Cuisine:** Indian | **Time:** 35 min | **Source:** Rainbow Plant Life
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mK3B0gjEjRc

**Ingredients:**
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2-inch ginger, grated
- 1 serrano pepper, diced (optional)
- 1 tsp each: turmeric, cumin, kosher salt
- ½ tsp each: coriander, chili powder
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1-1½ cups red lentils, rinsed
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- 1 can coconut milk
- 3 tbsp almond butter
- Juice of ½ lemon
- ½ cup cilantro
- Rice for serving

**Steps:**
1. Rinse lentils until water runs clear.
2. Heat oil, cook garlic + ginger + serrano 2 min.
3. Add all spices, cook 30-60 sec until fragrant.
4. Add lentils, broth, tomatoes. Boil, then simmer 20-25 min until lentils are soft.
5. Stir in coconut milk and almond butter, cook 5 more min.
6. Off heat, add lemon juice and cilantro. Serve over rice.

### 19. Quick Butter Chicken
**Cuisine:** Indian | **Time:** 30 min | **Source:** Joshua Weissman
**YouTube:** https://youtu.be/mrDJ2K3JXsA

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 lb chicken thighs, bite-sized pieces
- ¾ cup yogurt
- 2½ tsp Kashmiri chili powder (or paprika + cayenne)
- 2 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp each: turmeric, cumin, salt
- 1 tbsp garam masala
- 2-inch ginger, grated
- 2 tbsp ghee (or butter)
- 1 onion, sliced
- 5 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 can crushed tomatoes
- ¾ cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Basmati rice, cilantro

**Steps:**
1. Marinate chicken in yogurt + spices + ginger (even 5 min helps).
2. Heat ghee in skillet. Sear chicken until golden. Remove.
3. Cook onion, garlic, ginger until soft. Add spices + sugar.
4. Add tomatoes, cook 2 min. Blend until smooth.
5. Add cream + chicken back. Simmer covered 5-7 min.
6. Off heat, stir in 2 tbsp butter. Serve over rice with cilantro.

### 20. Falafel + Hummus + Tahini Sauce
**Cuisine:** Middle Eastern | **Time:** 30 min active | **Source:** Basics with Babish
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-98r0kpLbIM

**Note:** Requires overnight chickpea soaking. Use dried chickpeas, NOT canned.

**Falafel Ingredients:**
- 225g dried chickpeas (soaked overnight in salted water)
- 1 tbsp cumin, 2 tsp coriander, ¼ tsp Aleppo pepper
- 2 garlic cloves
- 1 small onion, diced
- ¼ cup parsley, 3 tbsp dill, 3 tbsp mint
- 2 tsp lemon zest
- 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 3½ tbsp flour
- Oil for cooking

**Hummus:** 12 oz canned chickpeas + 2 garlic + lemon juice + ⅓ cup tahini + ½ cup olive oil + ¼ cup ice water + salt

**Tahini Sauce:** ½ cup tahini + ¼ cup lemon juice + ½ tsp cumin + 1 garlic + ¼ cup ice water + salt

**Steps:**
1. Drain soaked chickpeas. Toast spices in dry pan. Pulse everything in food processor (not a paste). Add baking powder + flour.
2. Shape into balls, refrigerate 30 min.
3. Bake at 375°F for 15-20 min, flipping halfway (easiest for beginners).
4. Blend hummus ingredients until smooth.
5. Whisk tahini sauce ingredients.
6. Serve in pita with hummus, tahini sauce, veggies.

### 21. Poblano Con Queso Tacos
**Cuisine:** Mexican | **Time:** 15 min | **Source:** Ethan Chlebowski
**YouTube:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UIOW18kRDEA (starts at 4:30)

**Ingredients:**
- 2 poblano peppers
- 1 block Monterey Jack cheese, cut into sticks
- 1 cup grape tomatoes
- ½ onion
- 1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
- Handful fresh cilantro
- Juice of 1 lime
- Salt
- 3 corn tortillas
- Oil

**Steps:**
1. Set oven to high broil. Remove tops/seeds from poblanos.
2. Stuff peppers with cheese sticks. Place on sheet pan with tomatoes and onion. Drizzle oil. Broil 5 min.
3. Blend roasted onion + tomatoes + chipotle + cilantro + lime + salt into salsa.
4. Chop roasted peppers. Cook in skillet until cheese melts and crisps. Lay tortillas on top, cover to steam 1-2 min.
5. Flip tortillas onto plates, top with cheesy peppers and salsa.

## Beginner Tips

- **Day-old rice** is essential for fried rice — fresh rice turns mushy
- **High heat** is the key to stir-frying — get your pan very hot before adding food
- **Prep everything before cooking** (mise en place) — stir-fries move fast
- **"Al dente"** means pasta still has slight firmness, not mushy
- **Reserved pasta water** is starchy water saved before draining — helps sauces cling
- **Always let meat rest** a few minutes after cooking before cutting
- **Dark soy sauce ≠ regular soy sauce** — dark is thicker and adds color, regular is saltier
- **Internal temperatures:** Chicken 165°F/74°C, Beef (medium) 145°F/63°C
